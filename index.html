Explore festivals

Japan is a country of festivals. Any occasion can be a cause for celebration. Witness the changing of the seasons or the harvesting rice

Food diversity

The Japanese culinary scene is stepped in tradition. From famous dishes such as Sushi, Miso and Ramen, but also dishes only available in certain cities and regions.

Stunning nature

As Japanese archipelago stretches north and south, having many islands, mountains and valleys that split the land, a variety of natural environments are observed.
